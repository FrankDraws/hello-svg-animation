About Me

Photoshop, Illustrator, and Coda are a few of the many tools that come easy to me.

My attention to detail comes naturally; I love type, color matters a great deal, and have an unhealthy obsession with spacing.

Compositions have to be balanced in one way or another. Designs must have integrity, whether it’s for print or digital.

I have a great sense of humor.

Usability, simplicity and consistency are always at the root of my designs. If it’s not simple to use, it’s not good design. Just being pretty doesn’t cut it for me.

Won’t hesitate to share ideas about how to make a product better even if they fall outside of my expertise.

Won’t pout if my ideas aren't chosen and realize that being part of the process is essential to a great outcome.

Able to learn just about any technology, be it applications or web languages.

I enjoy coding as much as design. It's all an art.

I find satisfaction in passing on what I’ve learned by mentoring team members.
